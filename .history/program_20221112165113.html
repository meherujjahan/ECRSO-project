The Focus of Our Efforts

​Children Rights and Education

Most of our efforts pertaining to this program involve protecting
poor children’s rights. Because today’s children are the future
leader of the world. Children's rights include the right to
health, education, family life, play and recreation, an adequate
standard of living and to be protected from abuse and harm.

​Environment Protection

Through our project , we have the potential to make a real and
positive change in the environment . This is one of our key areas of
focus here at Environment & Children Rights Save Organisation,
and a source of much success for our organisation. Get in touch
with us today and see how you can lend a helping hand with this program.

Community Development

We see every challenge as an opportunity, and this initiative helps
us ensure that our partners are better prepared to manage the
unique situations they find themselves in. We are invested in an
innovative approach that empowers our community and delivers
the support they need, when they need it.
